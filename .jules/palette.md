## 2024-05-22 - Icon-only Button Accessibility
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like Edit/Delete actions in lists) are frequently missing `aria-label` attributes, making them inaccessible to screen reader users who only hear "button".
**Action:** Systematically check all icon-only buttons for `aria-label` during development and code reviews.
